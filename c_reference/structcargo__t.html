char cargo_t::unit[32]

Cargo-specific unit of amount.

Every cargo is internally amounted in Cargo Units. However, cargos may specify their own unit to display to the player.

If 'unit' is an empty string (where the first byte is a null byte, and thus strlen(unit) is zero), the amount displayed should be in regular Cargo Units. The usual denomination of Cargo Unist is in cubic decimeters (which is to say, a litre). This may vary, depending on the localization.
